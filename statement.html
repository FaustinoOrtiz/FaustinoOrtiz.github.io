Artist Statement

My name is Faustino Ortiz or you can just call me Tino for short. As a artist, the main things that interest me are video games. I'm from San Francisco and I am currently pursuing a career in the video game industry. This has been my dream goal since I was young and I am hoping to achieve this dream soon. As you will soon see most of my art projects are related to video games in some shape or form. I have always believed that if you do a project that is something that interests you and that your are passionate about then the quality of your work will definitely show how much you like these subjects. As a result my passion for gaming has never been stronger and is what propels me to have a career in the Gaming Industry. The way I tried to incoroprate games into my works include topics that could have a huge impact on the industry as a whole. For example for my video titled The Brief Awareness of a Violent Video Game Tax it talks about the possibility of the government implementing a tax on games that could be demmed to violent in the hands of kids. I also try to implement games in other forms as well. Some of which include making a processing sketch that includes a character's image and their theme song or even a hand drawing. If games help fit the message that I want my viewer to understand then I will implement them the best way possible.
