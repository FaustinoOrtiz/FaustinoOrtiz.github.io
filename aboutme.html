Statement of Purpose

My purpose is to create work that demonstrates the kind of person I am and what my interests are. By doing so it helps the viewer get to know me a little bit more. Not only that but its helps explain why I would chose certain topics for my work and what message I am trying to get across, because my passion is so high for that subject that the quality of my work that I do will show for it.
